About Me

Hi! I'm Isabella Rivera, a passionate student exploring the intersection of public health and technology. I believe that health access and education should be equitable, empowering, and easy to navigate, especially for underserved communities. Through my projects, I aim to create digital tools that are not only functional but also warm, user-friendly, and inclusive.

Whether it's designing an app for chronic illness support or building a website to motivate study routines, my goal is to make people feel seen, supported, and successful. This portfolio is a reflection of that mission, combining code, compassion, and creativity into solutions that make a difference.
